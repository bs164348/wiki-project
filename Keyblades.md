# Keyblades

Keyblades are mysterious weapons shaped like keys and are central to the Kingdom Hearts series.

They are wielded by chosen individuals and have the power to fight darkness, unlock paths, and seal worlds. Each Keyblade has a unique design and abilities, often reflecting the heart of its wielder.

Keyblades are not just weapons—they are deeply connected to the heart, making them powerful symbols of light, darkness, and balance.
